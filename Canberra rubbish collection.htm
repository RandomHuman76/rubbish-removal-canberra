Disposal of Old Mattresses

Fortunately, at CBR Rubbish Removal, we know what to do. We can safely pick up and remove your bed frames and mattresses, without damage to yourself or your walls, floors and doors. If possible, we will recycle what we can, provided they match certain criteria, and dispose of what we cant recycle in the appropriate locations.​

 

Did you know just how much of a mattress can be recycled? 70 percent of it, in fact.

 

You have the springs, which can be melted down by scrap metal dealers and used in building and roofing solutions, cars and appliances.

 

Then there's the foam and latex which are supposed to make you feel like you are lying on a cloud (only now, the 'cloud' might feel a bit lumpy). They can be recycled into products like carpet underlay.

 

And any recovered timber becomes woodchips, and perhaps ironically, what was once a part of your bedding, can now be used in making animal bedding.

Do not take your mattresses to the dump and just leave them there to rot. Let us handle the mattress disposal quickly and easily by taking them to a Mattress Recycling Centre.
